STPD

Source, Transform, Perfect, Drive

Our Cause

This group is based around the racing simulator called Assetto Corsa. Assetto Corsa has grown in popularity for its modding community. Many great cars and tracks have come from the Assetto Corsa Modding Community itself. What makes STPD stand out is it strives for absolute perfection. Whether it is down to the textures of the stitches, STPD prides itself in recreating the most accurate and visually appealing car mods in Assetto Corsa.

Services

Our services are on a commission basis. Pricing heavily depends on the complexity of the car and level of detail desired. STPD prides itself in accuracy of the model to its real life counterpart. This usually means heavy modification and re-texturing of the raw model from Polyphony Digital. Inquiries are free but a down payment of atleast 100 dollars is recquired.
